PigeonPost
Clipboard & Files,
iPhone to Windows

PigeonPost is a lightweight Windows tray app that lets your iPhone instantly sync clipboard content and transfer files — directly over your local network, with no cloud service or account required.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A focused set of capabilities that work reliably, right on your local network — no internet dependency for core functionality.

📋

Clipboard Sync

Copy text on your iPhone and paste it on Windows — or the reverse. Instant, bidirectional, no intermediary service.

📁

File Transfer

Send photos, videos, PDFs, and any other document straight to your Windows Downloads folder in one tap from your iPhone.

🔍

Auto-Discovery

Bonjour/mDNS advertisement means PigeonPostCompanion finds your PC automatically on the local network — no IP address to type.

🔒

Bearer Token Auth

Optionally protect every API call with a cryptographically random bearer token. Enable, disable, or regenerate it from the Settings dialog.

🌐

Remote Access

Works seamlessly over Tailscale, giving you secure access from anywhere without opening firewall ports or configuring a VPN from scratch.

📤

Share Sheet Extension

PigeonPostCompanion registers a Share Sheet extension — send files directly from Photos, Files, Safari, or any other iOS app.

🖥️

Unobtrusive Tray App

PigeonPost lives quietly in the Windows system tray with a colour-coded status icon. Close the window and it keeps running in the background.

🔄

Auto-Update

The Windows app checks for new releases at startup and every 24 hours. A one-click in-app banner downloads and installs updates instantly.

🌙

Dark & Light Mode

Full Windows 11 Mica Material theming with Light, Dark, and "Follow Windows" options. The iOS companion respects your device appearance setting.

Getting Started

Up and running in three steps

From download to your first file transfer in under five minutes.

1

Install PigeonPost on Windows

Download the installer from GitHub Releases and run it. The app starts immediately and registers itself in the system tray — no configuration wizard required.

2

Install the iOS Companion

Download PigeonPostCompanion from the App Store onto your iPhone or iPad. Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network, or connected via Tailscale.

3

Tap and Transfer

PigeonPostCompanion discovers your PC automatically via Bonjour. Tap it to connect, then start syncing clipboard content and sending files — instantly.

Privacy

Your data stays yours

PigeonPost is designed around a simple principle: what happens between your devices is nobody else's business.

🚫

No Data Collection

Neither the Windows app nor the iOS companion collects, stores, or transmits any personal data to external servers.

📡

Local Network Only

All clipboard and file transfers happen directly between your devices over your local network or Tailscale tunnel — never via a cloud service.

☁️

iCloud Settings Sync

The iOS companion stores your settings (server address, auth preferences) locally on-device. iOS may sync these to your personal iCloud account.

📊

No Analytics or Telemetry

There are no crash reporters, analytics SDKs, or telemetry pipelines in either app. No data about your usage is ever recorded.
